News

Two Swiss music publishers are being merged. BLACK BOX MUSIC (2014) is known for alternative electronic music from Switzerland, founded and run by Natalie Riede, who is deeply rooted in the electronic club scene. INTERSTELLAR MUSIC (2016) is a home for artists, their copyrights, and their careers, as well as the go-to place for music supervision for any film production. It's founded and managed by Marielène Froidevaux and producer Beat Jegen. It represents over 2000 titles of various genres created by artists from all over the world.

While Natalie is driving careers forward, opening doors for artists and brings in an enormous international network of the electronic music industry, Marielènes specialisations lie in secondary monetisation, rights management, licensing and supervision. With 20 years of industry experience, she has a broad network in the live music industry and the Swiss media world. With producer Beat Jegen and the Juna Recording Studios on their side, there is no holding back.

BLACK STELLAR MUSIC will be a place where artists, their music and their careers are looked after with love, passion and dedication. A place where customers and partners will find a solid and reliable supplier. A yes from these two is a one hundred percent commitment to the project.

Bringing together all these assets will open up countless opportunities for all artists represented and clients.
