Despite rejection of alchemy by artists in the Renaissance period (for instance, Leonardo da Vinci and Bernard Palissy) by period's workshop activities of manufacturing art, drugs and pigments can be difficult to separate from alchemical production. PHOTOGRAPHY

DREAMLIKE SOLITUDE_

The feeling of solitude is a constant companion when touring through the Alps by yourself or with a partner.
The pictures in this series have been shot on film during splitboarding in the spring in March in the Engadiner mountains, enjoying a silence which equally can't be found anywhere else.

In a conceptual fashion, the photographs have been scanned, repeated and mirrored to create a kaleidoscope-esque effect, which ultimately creates a new, dreamlike world. This new world can be seen and interpreted by each viewer differently, as everyone finds a new way to see and interpret the pictures.
